PassAI Account Deletion

If you wish to delete your PassAI account and all associated data, you have two options:

Option 1: Delete via the App (Automated)

You can instantly delete your account from within the PassAI mobile app:

  1. Open the PassAI app and log in.
  2. Navigate to your Profile.
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the prompt. This will permanently delete your data.

Option 2: Delete via Email Request

If you no longer have access to the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us.

Please send an email from the address associated with your PassAI account to:

ATOH044@GMAIL.COM

Include "Account Deletion Request" in the subject line. We will process your request and delete your account and associated data within 7 days.
